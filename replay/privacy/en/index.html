RePlay Privacy Policy

Last Update: 02-12-2023

What is RePlay and what does it do?

• RePlay is a simple music player application created by Unepix Team and still in development.

 

What data does RePlay collect on its end and for what purposes is it used?

• Since RePlay has only been providing offline service since 21-08-2023, that is, it only plays music (mp3, m4a..) files on your device, it does not collect any of your personal data.

 

Personalized ads

In-app ads are available as of 02-12-2023 and ad providers may collect/share your ad-related data.

 

App Permissions

The following permissions have been available in our application since 21-08-2023 and we have stated for what purpose we use this permission.

 

Internal Storage: Internal storage permission is only used to view/play music files on your device in the application.
